About us

Zoroastrian Radio is an audio site for Behi Religion (Zoroastrianism). Our purpose is to make the audience familiar with the Zoroastrian religion and the right path. To make you better familiar with the bases, in a big part of our programs we will use the words of great Zoroastrian Priests and famous writers and researchers.

Radio shows that broadcast from Zoroastrian Radio are not related with Avagah Radio and they are completely independent. Each of the speakers are responsible for their speaks and opinions.
